FACE 2 FACE

Flyers for @Lullaby.yvr special face 2 face event.

Rave Culture A Face 2 Face in DJing is a live performance where two DJs play back-to-back on the same setup, taking turns or trading tracks in real time. It’s all about collaboration and improvisation, combining both DJs’ styles to create a dynamic and unpredictable set. The energy between the two performers often sparks unique transitions, live remixing, and spontaneous moments that wouldn’t happen in a solo set, giving the audience a one-of-a-kind experience.
